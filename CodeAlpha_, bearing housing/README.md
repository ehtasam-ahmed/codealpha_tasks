# ⚙️ Bearing Housing – 3D CAD Model

## 📖 Project Overview

This project focuses on the design and modeling of a **bearing housing** using CAD software. The main objective was to create a mechanically accurate and manufacturable component that can support and hold a ball bearing in position while allowing smooth rotational motion of a shaft.

The model was developed based on standard engineering practices, including proper dimensioning, symmetry, and realistic assembly structure. A detailed 2D drawing and high-quality rendered model were also generated to visualize the final design.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Design a functional bearing housing component
* Apply accurate dimensions and geometric constraints
* Ensure proper fit for the bearing inside the housing
* Create a detailed 2D engineering drawing
* Generate a realistic 3D rendered model

---

## 🛠️ Software Used

* SolidWorks / AutoCAD / Creo (whichever you used)
* Rendering Tool (SolidWorks Visualize or similar)

---

## 🧩 Design Description

The designed component is a **pedestal-type bearing housing** consisting of a semi-circular support structure mounted on a flat base.

* The **central circular cavity** is designed to fit a ball bearing securely
* The **outer curved structure** provides strength and load support
* The **base plate** ensures stable mounting on a surface
* Two **bolt holes** are provided on both sides for proper fastening
* Fillets and smooth edges are used to reduce stress concentration

The design ensures proper alignment of the shaft and minimizes vibration during operation.

---

## 📐 Key Dimensions (from drawing)

* Total length: 110 mm
* Height: ~62.5 mm
* Base width: 20 mm
* Bearing inner diameter: 22 mm
* Outer radius: 29.5 mm

(All dimensions are based on the provided engineering drawing)

## ⚙️ Material Selection

The component can be manufactured using:

* Cast Iron (for vibration damping and strength)
* Mild Steel (for higher durability and load capacity)

Material choice depends on application requirements such as load, cost, and environment.

## 🧠 Design Considerations

* Proper **fit and tolerance** for bearing placement
* **Load distribution** through curved geometry
* **Ease of manufacturing** and assembly
* **Bolt hole positioning** for stable mounting
* Smooth geometry to reduce stress concentration
